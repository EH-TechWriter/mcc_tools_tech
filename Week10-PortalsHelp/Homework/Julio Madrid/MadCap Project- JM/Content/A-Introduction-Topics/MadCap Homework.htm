What is MadCap Flare?

MadCap Flare is a content management application used to create, manage, and publish content in a variety of formats.

Why Do Technical Writers Use MadCap Flare?

Here are two reasons technical writers and documentation teams use MadCap Flare:

  1. It publishes content to multiple outlets. Flare is a flexible tool that performs many functions across multiple platforms like e-pub and Salesforce.

  2. To maximize the reuse of content. Flare enables you to create derivatives without having to create duplicates of a source file, which saves professionals time and energy.
