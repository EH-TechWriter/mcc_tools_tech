Motorcycle Pre-Ride Safety Checks

A pre-ride motorcycle check is an often overlooked safety procedure. The pre-ride check of your motorcycle is as important as proper riding gear and seasonal maintenance.

This is not a comprehensive list, but a good starting guide for making the pre-ride check a normal routine before embarking on any ride.

  • Horn and turn signals

  • Kickstand

  • Lights

  • Fluid leaks

  • Tires and Tire Inflation

  • VIN Number Verification
