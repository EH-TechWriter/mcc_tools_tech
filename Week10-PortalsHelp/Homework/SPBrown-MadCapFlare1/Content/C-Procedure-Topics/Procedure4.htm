Tires and Inflation

Proper tire inflation ensures that the tires will perform as intended under speed and load. Over-inflated tires can cause blowouts (if the tires are not run-flats) and overheating. Under-inflation can also cause overheating, tire rollover under heavy cornering and cause unresponsive turning on the front tire.

The tires should be checked for uneven wear, cracks, and punctures.
