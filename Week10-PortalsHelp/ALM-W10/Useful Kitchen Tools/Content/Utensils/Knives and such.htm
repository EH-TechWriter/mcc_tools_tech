Utensils

Again, some of the most useful utensils are really underrated and you don't realize how much you need them until you're without them.

 

Chef's Knife

  • Have this as a catch-all knife
  • Get a sharpening stone to prevent the knife from becoming dull

 

Slotted Spoon

  • Have at least one so you can scoop out foods from liquid
  • Really helpful for boiling to separate the water from the food

 

Ladle

  • For scooping liquids

 

Tongs

  • These are a game changer
  • Great for turning when frying
  • Get one with a nonstick covering so the metal part of the tongs won't be damaged from touching the pan

 

Whisk

  • Important for baking

 

Spatula

  • Two different kinds:
    • A spatula with a flat surface bent at an angle: used for picking up and moving food around
    • Silicone spatula: used for scraping wet foods off the sides of bowls and pans

 

Cutting board

  • People usually get one designated for meat and another for everything else
  • Make sure it is heavy enough for it to stay on the table while you cut, or that it has some type of grip surface
