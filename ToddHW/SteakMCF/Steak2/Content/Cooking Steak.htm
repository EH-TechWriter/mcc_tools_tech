Cooking Steak

  1. Preheat oven to 375°. Place cast iron skillet in the oven while it is preheating and for an additional 10 minutes.
  2. Remove cast iron pan from the oven, add a touch of olive oil to the pan, and place on a burner at high heat.
  3. Salt the steaks and add to the cast iron skillet. Sear each side for 3 minutes and then place the skillet with the steaks in it in the oven to finish cooking.
  4. Remove the steaks from the oven and skillet and let rest for 10 minutes.
  5. Check the temperature of the steaks to ensure proper doneness.
