Cuts of Steak

The cuts of steaks vary greatly, especially in marbling and tenderness, but also in flavor that is often affected by the cow breed and how it was raised.

A trusted brand of steaks is Certified Angus Beef<tm>, which is beef from the Angus breed and USDA graded Choice or Prime. Certified Black Angus<tm> requires additional quality specifications to be met related to marbling and tenderness.
