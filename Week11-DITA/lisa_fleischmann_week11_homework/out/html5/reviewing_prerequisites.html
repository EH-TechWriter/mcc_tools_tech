Reviewing Prerequisites

When reviewing prerequisites for Acme Eye, it is important to review the web browsers and software that Acme Eye supports.

Acme Eye supports web browsers and software, as shown in the table below.

Item Version
Firefox 17 or later
Chrome 12 or later
Safari 4 or later
Windows 7 or later
Mac OS 7 or later
Microsoft Office 2003 or later
