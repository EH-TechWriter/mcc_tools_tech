Completing Data Screens
Once you have created your project in Acme Eye, complete data screens, including Acme Prime and Customer.
To complete the Acme Prime data screen, follow these steps:
From the Document Outline, select Acme Prime .
Enter your Acme Prime information, as follows. Figure 4-1 shows an example of a completed Acme Prime data screen.
First Name: Your first name.
Last Name: Your last name.
Title: Your job title.
Address: Your address.
Phone Number: Your phone number.
Email Address: Your email address.
