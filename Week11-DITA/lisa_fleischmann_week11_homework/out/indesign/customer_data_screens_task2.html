Completing Customer Data Screens
Once you have created your project in Acme Eye, complete data screens, including Acme Prime and Customer.
To complete the Customer data screen, follow these steps:
From the Document Outline, select Customer.
Enter the Customer information, as follows.
First Name: Customer first name.
Last Name Customer last name.
Company: Customer company.
Address: Customer address.
Phone Number: Customer phone number.
Email Address: Customer email address.
Click Next.
